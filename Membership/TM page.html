WHAT IS AIESEC?
WHAT CAN YOU DO WITH AIESEC
AIESEC offers an environment similar to a start-up in which our members do activities related to sales, marketing and management, where transferable business skills can be developed regardless of academic background.
Very! We work together in teams to develop ourselves and the world. Until it's safe we will regularly meet virtually. Becoming an AIESECer gives you the chance to connect with AIESECers around the world as we are based in 114+ countries.
Present in 2 Universities across the Latvia our membership is made up of youth from various nationalities. We work together and learn from each other. We embrace our differences as a community and strive to adopt a global mindset in everything we do.

Special Unit President (SUP)
  • Responsible for the Maintaining and the legality of the entity at the University.
  • External Representation of the Unit (Entity) at any events in and outside the school that aligns to AIESEC values.
  • Development of Unit direction guideline and strategy for their overall growth.
  • Presentation of the Unit at the Board of president meeting.
  • Responsible for management of any financial asset of the Unit (entity).
  • Responsible directly communicate about recent development and activities in the Unit to the Member committee through the expansions manager & Board of President (BoP).

Special Unit Vice President for Business to Customers (SUVP B2C)
  • Responsible for developing strategies to promote exchange programs and support potential exchange participants.
  • Downscale national outgoing exchange promotion strategies at the local level.
  • Develop a workable marketing strategy for the Unit (entity).
  • Downscale Incoming exchange knowledge to the Unit (entity).
  • Carry out on ground and online marketing activities for the Unit.
  • Develop a communication strategy for members engagement.
  • Downscale national strategies at the local level and educate members of the recent development in AIESEC platforms.
  • Notify members about recent events like conferences, youth speak forums and surveys.

Special Unit Vice President for Business to Business (SUVP B2B)
  • Responsible for developing sales strategies for any incoming exchange product assigned to the Unit by the member committee (national body).

  • Implement national incoming exchange sales strategies according to guidelines from the national body.
